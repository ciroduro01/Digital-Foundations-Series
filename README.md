## The Mission

In a world driven by technology, digital literacy is no longer an optional skillâ€”it is a fundamental right. **Digital Foundations** was created to bridge the gap between simply "using a computer" and truly "mastering" one.

These activities have been **tested in the classroom** and refined to be accessible, fun, and highly educational. By observing how learners interact with these tasks, the content has been polished to ensure maximum engagement.

---

## Volume 1: Hardware & Word Processing

**Target Audience:** Beginners Classes (Year 1)

This workbook consists of 120 practical activities designed to be completed in 10 to 15 minutes each, following a **"Learning by Doing"** philosophy.

### Essential Pillars Covered in Vol 1:

* **Hardware Mastery:** Identifying what's under the hood.
* **Software & Navigation:** Moving beyond the desktop.
* **The Researcher's Mindset:** Ethical and efficient web searching.
* **Typography & Design:** The art of digital communication.
